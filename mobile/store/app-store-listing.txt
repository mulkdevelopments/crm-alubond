DESCRIPTION
Alubond CRM helps the Alubond sales team manage construction project pipeline, follow-ups, team progress, and site visits from iPhone and iPad.

• View and update project pipeline stages
• Track project value, location, and commercial details
• Log activities, site visits, and follow-ups
• See team assignments and project map
• Role-based access for reps, managers, and admins

Alubond CRM is for authorized Alubond team members only.
